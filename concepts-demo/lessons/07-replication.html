Replication & Redundancy

Redundancy eliminates single points of failure. Replication keeps data consistent across copies. Together they enable high availability.

Redundancy vs Replication

Redundancy

Extra capacity ready for failover. Hot standby servers, multiple network links, backup power.

Replication

Copying data across nodes to keep consistent. Master-slave, multi-master, sync vs async.

Quorum Reads/Writes

In leaderless replication, a quorum ensures overlap between read and write sets.

W + R > N

W = write replicas, R = read replicas, N = total replicas
